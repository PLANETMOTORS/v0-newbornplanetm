## Step 8: Q&A Management

### Pre-populate Common Questions

**Q: Do you offer financing for bad credit?**
A: Yes! We work with multiple lenders to help customers with all credit situations. Most applications are approved within minutes. Apply online at www.planetmotors.ca/financing

**Q: Do you deliver vehicles?**
A: Yes, we offer nationwide delivery across Canada. Most vehicles are delivered within 5-7 business days. Delivery fees vary by location.

**Q: What's included in your inspection?**
A: Every vehicle undergoes our 210-point inspection covering mechanical, electrical, safety, and cosmetic components. EV vehicles also receive a battery health certification.

**Q: What's your return policy?**
A: We offer a 10-day/1,000km money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, return the vehicle for a full refund.

**Q: Are your prices negotiable?**
A: Our prices are transparent and competitive. We use market data to price vehicles fairly, so what you see is what you pay - no haggling required.
