## Executive Summary

Clutch.ca is a major competitor in the Canadian online used car market with:
- **1.39M monthly visits** (July 2025)
- **Authority Score: 47/100**

**Key Finding:** 31% of Clutch's organic traffic comes from branded searches ("clutch", "clutch canada", "clutch.ca"). This presents an opportunity for Planet Motors to capture non-branded, high-intent keywords.
