## 4. Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

### FAQ Answer Format (40-60 words)
AI search engines extract answers that:
- Start with "Yes" or "No" when applicable
- Include specific numbers/stats
- Mention local context (Ontario, Canada)
- End with a value proposition

**Example:**
> Q: Is the Toyota RAV4 good for Ontario winters?
> 
> A: Yes! The RAV4's available All-Wheel Drive system, combined with 220mm 
> ground clearance and Toyota's stability control, makes it an excellent 
> choice for navigating snowy Ontario roads. The heated seats and mirrors 
> come standard, and the fuel-efficient hybrid option achieves 5.8 L/100km 
> combined.
